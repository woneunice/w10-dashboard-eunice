Objective
This dashboard answers one simple question: which ODI Grips Instagram content types perform best overall?

Approach
The goal is to make the results easy to understand for someone without a data or marketing analytics background.

What We’re Looking At
Not every type of Instagram content performs the same way. Some categories generate stronger engagement and interaction than others.

Why This Matters
Understanding these differences helps ODI Grips decide what kinds of posts to prioritize in future content planning.
